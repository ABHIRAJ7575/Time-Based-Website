## Features (Because apparently, telling time isn't enough)

*   **Hybrid Clock Section:** Because why settle for one way to tell time when you can have two aggressively synchronized ones? Digital digits for the pragmatists, and an analog sweep for the romantics (or those who just like watching things spin).
*   **World Time Dashboard:** Track time zones. Impress your international pen pals. Or just know when to call your cousin in Australia without waking them up at 3 AM. We even auto-detect your local zone, because we're thoughtful like that.
*   **Stopwatch:** For when you absolutely, positively need to time that instant noodle cooking competition down to the millisecond. Comes with lap listings, because every second counts in the noodle Olympics.
*   **Countdown Timer:** Perfect for counting down to that 'important' meeting, or more realistically, your next coffee break. Features a circular progress bar and a subtle "ðŸŽ‰ You did it!" confetti blast, because small victories deserve recognition.
*   **Pomodoro Timer:** Because even highly advanced beings need to be told when to work and when to stare blankly at a wall. Work and break cycles included, for optimal human (or cyborg) efficiency.
*   **Time Difference Calculator:** Finally, an answer to the age-old question: "Is it too late to call my friend who lives two time zones away to ask a really dumb question?" Now with fewer awkward apologies.
*   **Day Progress Section:** A constant, gentle reminder of how much day you've wasted... or conquered. You decide. Presented in a neon-accented circular bar, because even existential dread can be beautiful.
*   **Dynamic Greeting System:** It knows if you're a morning person or a night owl. It also judges your productivity silently.
*   **Thematic Magic:** Watch the UI magically transform from 'early morning hustle' gold-blue gradients to 'late-night coding session' purple-neon. No manual theme switching required, because who has time for that?
